How to Play Dominoes

Setup

28 domino tiles (0-0 to 6-6). Each player gets 10 tiles, 8 remain in the boneyard.

Starting

A random player starts by playing any tile from their hand.

Playing

Match one half of your tile to the head (left end) or tail (right end) of the chain. Click on a playable tile (highlighted in red) to play it.

Drawing

If you have no playable tile, click "Draw from Boneyard" to draw until you get a playable one or the boneyard is empty (then pass).

Scoring

  • If a player runs out of dominoes, they win the round and score points equal to the total pips in their opponent's hand.
  • If the game is blocked and no one can play, the player with the lowest total pips wins the round.

Winning

The first player to reach the target score (e.g., 100 or 150 points) over multiple rounds wins the game.
